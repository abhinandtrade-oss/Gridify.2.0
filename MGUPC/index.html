The Three Lifts

The ultimate test of total body strength

Squat

The King of Lifts. A test of lower body and core strength, requiring depth below parallel.

Bench Press

Pure upper body power. The bar must touch the chest and be pressed to full lockout.

Deadlift

The truest test of strength. Lifting dead weight from the floor to a standing position.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official M.G. University Inter Zone Powerlifting Championship website. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you register for the championship.

2. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Date of Birth and Age
  • University Register Number
  • College Name and Department
  • Gender and Weight Category
  • Emergency Contact Information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To process your registration for the Powerlifting Championship.
  • To verify your eligibility based on University rules.
  • To communicate event updates and schedules.
  • To ensure safety through emergency contact details.
  • To publish official results and rankings.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using GRIDIFY (a cloud database provided by GRIDIFY). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your data (this may affect your championship participation).
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact the Department of Physical Education, SSV College, or email us at mgupc@gridify.in.

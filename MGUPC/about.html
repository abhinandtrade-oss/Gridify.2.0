About the Organizers

Learn more about the institutions and the department behind this championship.

Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU)

Mahatma Gandhi University, established in 1983, is a premier center of higher learning in Kerala. Recognized as a Category-1 Autonomous University by the UGC and accredited with a prestigious NAAC A++ Grade, MGU is globally recognized for its academic rigor and research-led pedagogy.

National Ranking

Ranked 37th in NIRF 2024 (Impact Ranking) and consistently features among the top 50 universities in India, reflecting its commitment to quality education.

Global Research

Partners with international giants like Max Planck Institute (Germany), Caltech (USA), and Brown University for cutting-edge scientific and social research.

Innovation Hub

A powerhouse for intellectual property with numerous US and Indian patents, particularly in Chemical Sciences and Materials Research.

Sree Sankara Vidyapeetom (SSV) College

Guided by the motto "Thamaso ma jyothir gamaya" (Lead me from darkness to light), SSV College is a NAAC A++ Accredited (CGPA 3.59) institution managed by the Sree Sankara Trust. Established in 1967, it blends traditional Vedic values with modern technological advancements.

Academic Diversity

Offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across 13 departments, including specialized PG courses in Computer Science, History, and Clinical Chemistry.

Community & Heritage

Houses a unique Heritage Museum and conducts over 75+ annual extension activities, fostering strong social responsibility among students.

Skill Ecosystem

Home to active IEDC, Entrepreneurship Clubs, and IIC, empowering students through innovation fellowships and real-world industrial exposure.

Department of Physical Education

Our Vision

To engage in relentless pursuit of excellence in physical education and sports for health, fitness and well-being of the students and staff.

Our Mission

Quality teaching and coaching in cognitive psychomotor and affective domains in physical education and sports. All-round development through participation in diverse sports activities.

The Department of Physical Education has come a long way since its establishment in 1967. Within the liberal arts framework of SSV College, we offer instruction in individual, dual, and group activities that promote lifelong fitness. Our philosophy, "A sound mind in a sound body", drives us to implement new systems that balance elite sports performance with healthy lifestyle practices.

If exercise were a pill, it would be the most cost-effective drug ever invented. To this end, we boast a 4-acre sports center within our 18.5-acre campus, featuring a 200m 6-lane track, badminton courts, and a multi-stationed fitness center open to both the campus and the general public.

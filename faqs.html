Support & Services

Below, you will find answers to common questions regarding our post-launch support and service policies.

Pricing Package

Investing in a professional digital solution is a crucial step for business growth. At Gridify.in, we prioritize transparency, and this section is designed to clarify our approach to pricing. Since every project is uniquely tailored, the investment will reflect the specific features and complexity required. We encourage you to review these common questions to understand our payment terms, the factors that influence cost, and where to find our detailed package pricing.

The cost of your project is highly dependent on the scope, features, and level of customization required. Since we specialize in tailored solutions, we do not have a single fixed price. We encourage you to visit our Services page or Shop page, where you can explore our tiered package plans to get an immediate sense of the starting investment.

Several key factors influence the final cost. These include the required number of pages, the complexity of the UI/UX design, custom integrations (e.g., e-commerce functionality, third-party API connections), and the technology platform chosen. A simple informational site will cost less than a fully custom web application.

We typically require an initial deposit (often 50% of the total project fee) to secure your spot in our schedule and begin the design phase. The remaining balance is then due upon final approval of the completed website, just prior to launch. Specific payment milestones will always be clearly outlined in your personalized project proposal.

We strive for complete transparency. Our proposal includes all Gridify.in design and development fees. However, please note that ongoing costs such as domain registration, web hosting, and any necessary premium software licenses or third-party plugins are usually recurring costs paid directly by you to the respective service providers.

The initial project fee covers the one-time design, development, and launch of your website. It does not typically include ongoing maintenance, security monitoring, or content updates. For long-term peace of mind, we offer separate monthly or annual maintenance plans, which you can find detailed on our Service page.
